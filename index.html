The decisions that shape your life rarely have clean answers.

Crux is an AI decision coach built for adults navigating real choices. It helps you think through complex decisions with rigour, honesty, and a clear framework.

Download on the App Store Free at launch

Why Crux instead of a Chatbot?

Chatbots give answers. Crux helps you think. Where a general AI will happily tell you what to do, Crux uses a structured five-stage framework to draw out your own thinking, challenge your assumptions, and surface the trade-offs you're actually weighing.

Asks before it suggests
